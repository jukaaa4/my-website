HE TURNS HIS LIFE AROUND

Everything is possible. These are the words our teacher Mostafa (32 years) says to us every day, proving this by his own example. Born in Damascus and later moving with his parents and brothers from the capital of Syria to the province, Mostafa graduates from University and begins to work as a police officer. But, unfortunately, due to the outbreak of war in the country, the family has to leave their homeland. In the early years of emigration, Mostafa and his family live and work in Turkey. It was a very hard time for him and his family. Exactly in this time, his older brother persuades him to join and become a part of a large web developers family. This decision turns Mostafa whole subsequent life in 180 degrees. Being already in Germany, he works as a web developer and dreams to connect his life with Robotics. To achieve his goal he enters the Faculty of Robotics and Autonomous System at the University of Lübeck, where he is currently studying. In the future, his goal is the creation of robots and automated technical systems.

We, students of MAT 10, wanted to say you our dear Teacher: Thank you and Good luck in achieving your goals.
